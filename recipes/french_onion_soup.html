French onion soup

Care for a bageutte ?

To begin we need several ingredients :

  • Onion
  • Beef stock, or any stock
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Water
  • White wine
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Taste of europe, I did tried to make this once, taste okay. But I didn't have the real thing in France so...Let's do it anyway, let's begin:

  1. Cut the onion into thin slices
  2. Into the pot 2tbs of olive oil and Butter
  3. Melt the butter then add the onion
  4. On extreme low heat, stir constanstantly, not letting it sticks to the pot
  5. Cook until caramelized.
  6. Add white wine to deglaze the pot
  7. Bring it to a boil then simmer for 15 minutes, seasons to taste
  8. 2 slices of bread on top and grated Cheese
  9. Into the oven for 5 minutes on 180 Celcius, then serve.
