Pancake

Iconic american breakfast

To begin we need several ingredients :

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • Half tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • butter (melted,1 tbsp)

This should not be too hard, you can do this, with practice. You can even apply a job at Pancake house down the block ! Let's begin:

  1. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, and sugar together.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat egg and add melted butter.
  3. Mix together two bowl together.
  4. Heat up a pan with butter or cooking spray
  5. Pour the mixture in, medium heat is fine, wait until there're bubble on top of the surface
  6. flip the pancake.
  7. Cook the other side for a few minutes then serve.
  8. Serve with butter and maple syrup on top.

Pro-tip: Don't over mix the pancake batter, or the pancake will not as fluffy as we wanted it to be
