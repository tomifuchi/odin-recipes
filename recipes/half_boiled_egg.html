Half boiled egg

Firm on the outside and creamy on the inside

You can read alot on the internet for alot of tricks to half boil an egg, but to do so is really easy to me. Atleast I think it's. Get a stop watch or anything that can time moderately accurate.

Put the egg or eggs into a pot with cold water, high heat, bring them to a boil. Exactly 7 minutes turn off the heat, run them under tap for several minutes. Crack and enjoy.
