Seared salmon

Once peasants food, now a delicacy for everyone.

To begin we need several ingredients :

  • A piece of deboned salmon
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Moister is enemy to crispy, wash clean the salmon piece then dry them throughly. Let's begin

  1. Seasons the salmon with salt and pepper
  2. With a pan with hot oil, skin down lay the salmon inside the pan
  3. Now the fish should bulke and shrink, to prevent this so that the whole piece can evenly cook. Use a slotted spatula, press the salmon down. But gently, for 15 seconds.
  4. Use medium low heat to cook the salmon. Because the skin is naturally designed to insulates the insides. Rapid temperature increases causes the leaking of albumin, And uneven cooking. So medium low heat.
  5. Cook skin side down, about 90% completion then flip once
  6. Depends on your preference, 15 seconds or more on the other side. But remember that it's already 90% cooked, so don't spend too much time on the other side.
  7. Take out to a paper towel lined tray to remove excess oil. Then serve
