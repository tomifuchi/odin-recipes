Japanese rice curry

Unique spins of the English curry

To begin we need several ingredients :

  • A pack of potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Minced beef, Or any kind will do
  • Japanese curry cube
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Note: If you don't have Japanese curry cube, you can use the any curry substitute but it's highly recommended to use authentic Japanese curry cube for best flavor and accurate to the recipe.

This recipe should be simple, but simmering time should be the majority of the cooking time. Make time to watch and stir the pot occasionally not letting the curry burnt or stick to the bottom of the pot, let's begin:

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes in to 4 pcs.
  2. Peel and chop the carrots into 2cm slices
  3. Peel and slice the onion into 2cm slices
  4. Peel and smash 2 cloves of garlics
  5. Cut to bite size and seasons the beef with salt and pepper
  6. Pour 2tbs of oil into a medium pot
  7. Put the vegetables into the pot, stir fry until the onion is translucent and the garlic is golden
  8. Stir fry the beef, seers them to lock in the flavor.
  9. Pour water into the pot, covering the contents
  10. Bring them to a boil then, turn down the heat to simmer
  11. Simmer for 10minutes then put the curry cubes into the pot, stir well.
  12. Simmer for another 20 minutes, then seasons to taste, It should have a sauce consistency.
  13. Serve with rice on the side and pink ginger.

Pro-tip: Use authentic Japanese curry cubes for best flavor
