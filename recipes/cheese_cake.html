Cheese cake

Like cake ? Then you will enjoy cheese cake very much

To begin we need several ingredients :

  • Cream cheese (If you don't have this, follow instruction below)
  • Pack of biscuits
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Water

Have cream cheese ? If not follow these instrutions to make cream cheese. The ingredients to make cream cheese are:

  • Milk full fat
  • Cheese cloth
  • Lemon juice/Lime juice or plain vinegar

Directions to make cream cheese:

  1. Put your milk in a pot, high heat to almost boil.
  2. Lower heat to simmer the milk. Be sure to stir the milk occasionally to not let it get burnt
  3. Then slowly, add 1 tbs then stirs into the milk. You will see small clumps start to form, that's our curls seperating from the whey
  4. Continune to add few tbs as directed above slowly until you feel like it's enough. And the curls totally separated from the whey
  5. Strain the curls from the whey with a cheese cloth on top of a sift.
  6. Use a food processor make curls into cream cheese. Add salt to taste if needed.
Now if you already have cream cheese, let's make cheese cake:
  1. Put the biscuits into a food processor, blend it to your liking. THis is going to be your bottom crust of the cheese cake. Coarse or fine whatever you like.
  2. Next, with a baking tray, spread your biscuits on there.
  3. Now put your cream cheese into a bowl, add 1/2 cup of sugar, or more if you like sweets
  4. Add 4 eggs into it, then mix the cream cheese mixture to incorperates it.
  5. Pour the mixture into the baking tray with the biscuits crust under
  6. Pour water around the outside of the baking tray to not let the cheese cake dry up
  7. With 180 Celcius oven, bake the whole thing in about an hour
  8. Serve ! You can eat this with strawberries, bananas. Anything, go crazy.
